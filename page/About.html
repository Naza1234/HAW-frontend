Working with equinest

Great knowledge

With many years of experience in the horse world, our team, Sjef Janssen & Inigo Astiz Tabar, knows a lot about the industry. We're experts in buying and selling. We understand all about dressage horses, how they are trained, and what you might need. We will find you the perfect horse.

Precise Selection Process

We carefully pick each horse in our collection. Our team of experts check how they look, what they've learned, and if they're ready to perform for any level rider. We check all the info we get to make sure it's correct. We want our clients to have the real facts about each horse,

Outstanding Legacy

Anky van Grunsven, a respected dressage icon, has partnered with Equinest. Her commitment to excellence aligns with our mission. Anky's guidance ensures that every horse we represent meets the highest standards of quality.

Sjef Janssen, is a Dutch equestrian legend renowned for his expertise in dressage. As a top trainer and coach, he played a vital role in the success of several Olympic and World Championship medalists. He also has been chef d'equipe for The Netherlands and Belgium which has earned him widespread admiration and acclaim in the global equestrian community.

Impeccable Reputation

At Equinest, we're known for reliabilty in the horse trading world. We value good relationships with breeders, trainers, and riders. This helps us provide top-notch horses with a proven track record, making us a trusted choice in the horse community.
